Cuccuma: A redesign for my local coffee house in Bergmannkiez, Berlin

PROJECT TYPE

Graphic Design

MEDIA

Brand Identity & Print & Enviromental

DATE

2022

BRAND ATTRIBUTES

Playful | Cosy | Contemporary

Cuccuma is a coffee shop with a fun, relaxed and homey vibe, and has become one of the most popular hangout spots in my local neighbourhood of Bergmannkiez, Berlin. I imagined this rebrand whilst nursing several of their delicious cappuccinos. For this coffee house, I designed a new logo, outdoor sign, window sign, menus, coffee bean packaging, coffee cups, and bakery packaging.

A Graphic & UX/UI Designer based in Berlin specialising in Brand Identity, Digital, User Experience, Print, Editorial, & Packaging.
