ABOUT ME
Hey! I'm Penelope Atkinson, or Penny to my friends. I'm a Berlin-based Graphic Designer and UX Designer, with a background in Data Analysis and Customer Service. I have a broad skillset across project management, user experience design, brand identity, print & editorial design, and digital design for websites and apps. I enjoy creating designs that find a sweet balance between playfulness and functionality, and I'm especially interested to work with brands that have a positive social and environment impact. In my free time, you'll find me on a weekend nature adventure, playing volleyball or swimming, or arranging my next social hang-out. Have a role that I could be a good fit for? I'm currently looking for a full-time position as a Graphic Designer at a Berlin-based design agency. Would love to hear from you!
